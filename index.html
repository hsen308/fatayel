// The Spot

Made on
the corner.

No frills, no waiting around — just fatayel and shawarma done right, every day of the week except Monday.

Fatayel opened on Bshara El Khoury with one job: feed the street fast, feed it well. The dough's rolled fresh, the makanek's grilled to order, and the shawarma spins all day long.

It's a stand-up, grab-and-go kind of place — order at the counter, watch it get made, and you're back on the street in minutes. Lebanon's favorite street food, done properly.
